Predict

1RM Estimator — Privacy Policy — Last updated: 2026-07-16

Summary: Predict does not collect, store, or transmit personal data to external servers. All user-entered data stays on your device.

1. Data stored on your device

Predict stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Last entered weight, reps, and RPE values (to pre-fill the form)
  • Unit preference (kg / lbs) and rounding preference
  • Estimation history (up to 50 entries)

This data is not transmitted to any server and is not accessible to the developer.

2. Crash reporting

Predict uses Sentry for anonymous crash and error reporting.

  • No personal data is included in crash reports
  • Reports contain: stack trace, Android version, device model, app version
  • Data is hosted in the EU (Frankfurt, Germany)
  • Legal basis: legitimate interest (app stability)
  • Data processor: Sentry — sentry.io/privacy

3. Advertising

Predict displays ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may use your device's advertising ID to show relevant ads.

  • A consent dialog is shown before personalised ads are displayed (GDPR)
  • You can withdraw or change your consent at any time in the app settings
  • You can opt out of personalised ads in your device settings
  • AdMob privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

4. No account required

Predict does not require registration or login. No email address, name, or other personal identifier is collected.

5. Children

Predict is intended for users aged 16 and older. The app is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect data from minors.

6. Your rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the right to access, correct, or delete personal data. Since Predict stores data only on your device, you can delete all app data via Android Settings → Apps → Predict → Clear Data.

For crash report data held by Sentry, contact us using the link below.

7. Contact

For privacy-related questions, please use the GitHub Issues page.
