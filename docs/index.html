Predict

1RM Estimator — Privacy Policy — Last updated: 2026-07-10

Summary: Predict does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data stays on your device.

1. What data the app uses

Predict stores the following data locally on your device only, using Android SharedPreferences:

  • Your last entered weight and reps (to pre-fill the form)
  • Your RPE setting
  • Your unit preference (kg / lbs)
  • Your rounding preference (rounded / raw)
  • Estimation history (up to 50 entries)

This data never leaves your device and is not shared with anyone.

2. Crash reporting

Predict uses Sentry for crash and error reporting. If the app crashes, an anonymous error report is sent to Sentry's servers (hosted in the EU — Frankfurt, Germany).

  • No personal data is included in crash reports
  • No user identifiers, names, or email addresses are collected
  • Reports contain: stack trace, Android version, device model, app version
  • Data processor: Sentry (sentry.io/privacy)
  • Legal basis: legitimate interest (app stability)
  • Data transfer: EU Standard Contractual Clauses (SCC)

3. Advertising

The free version of Predict displays ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may use your device's advertising ID to show relevant ads.

  • You will be asked for consent before ads are shown (GDPR consent dialog)
  • You can opt out of personalised ads at any time in your device settings
  • Pro users do not see ads
  • AdMob privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

Note: AdMob integration is planned for a future version. This section will be updated when it goes live.

4. In-App Purchases

Predict offers an optional one-time Pro upgrade via Google Play In-App Billing. Purchase and payment data is handled entirely by Google Play. Predict does not receive or store any payment information.

5. No account required

Predict does not require registration or login. No email address, phone number, or other personal identifier is ever requested.

6. Children

Predict is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Your rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal data we hold. Since Predict stores data only on your device, you can delete all app data at any time via Android Settings → Apps → Predict → Clear Data.

For crash report data held by Sentry, contact us at the address below.
